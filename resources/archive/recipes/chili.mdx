# McSorley's Impossible Chili

![McSorley's Impossible Chili](../../images/food/mcsorleys.jpg)

I adapted this recipe from an old newspaper article hanging on the wall at McSorley's Old Ale House in the East Village.

<RecipeComponent>
[1] Heat: 3 tbsp peanut oil, 3 tbps butter
[2] Fry until translucent: 1 large red onion, 4 cloves garlic, #1
[3] Cook:  1 1/2 lbs Impossible meat, #2
[4] Season: 3 tbps chili powder, #3
[5] Simmer for 1hr: 1 can (~4cups) diced tomatoes, 2 tsp cumin, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp tabasco, 1/2 tsp salt, #4
[6] Add & bring back to a simmer: 2 cups kidney beans, 1 cup chick peas, #5
</RecipeComponent>
