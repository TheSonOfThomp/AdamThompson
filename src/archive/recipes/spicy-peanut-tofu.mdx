# Spicy Peanut Tofu

A great, easy to make vegan dish. 

![Spicy peanut tofu](../../images/food/spicy-tofu.jpg)

<RecipeComponent>
[1] Dry & dice (~1 inch cubes):
  1 lb tofu
[2] Chop: 1/2 red onion
[3] Mince: 4 cloves garlic
[4] Mix and microwave for 30 seconds:
  2 tbps rice vinegar,
  2 tbsp peanut butter,
  2 tbsp ketchup,
  1 tsp sesame oil,
  1 tsp soy sauce,
  1/4 tsp cayenne pepper,
  1/4 tsp black pepper,
  1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
[5] Prepare:
  2 cups rice (or 1/2 pack spaghetti noodles)
[6] Mix:
  #1, 1 tbsp corn starch
[7] Fry medium-high until golden-brown:
  3 tbps canola oil, #6
[8] Fry until translucent:
  #7, #2, #3
[9] Fry about 2 minutes:
  #8, 2 vegetables (pepper or broccoli or zucchini)
[10] Mix: #4, #9
[11] Combine: #5, #10
</RecipeComponent>
