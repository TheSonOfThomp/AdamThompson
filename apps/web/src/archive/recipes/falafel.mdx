# Falafel

Best paired with Tzatziki and [Tabbouleh](/recipes/tabbouleh)

![Falafel with Dill sauce and Tabbouleh](../../images/food/falafel.jpg)

<RecipeComponent>
[1] Mash: 2 cans (~32oz) drained chickpeas
[2] Dice: 1 onion
[3] Blend in food processor: #1, #2, 1/2 cup parsley, 3 cloves garlic
[4] Knead in: #3, 2 eggs, 2 tsp cumin, 1 1sp coriander, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1/8 tsp cayenne, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1 tsp baking powder, 2 tsp olive oil, 1 cup bread crumbs
[5] Roll into ~1 inch round pucks: #4
[6] Fry in cast iron on medium heat, about 3 mins per side: #5, 1/4 - 1/2 inch cooking oil in pan
