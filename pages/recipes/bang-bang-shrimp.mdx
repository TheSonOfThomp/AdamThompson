# Bang Bang shrimp

![Bang Bang shrimp](../../images/food/bang-bang-shrimp.jpeg)

<Recipe>
[1] Thaw: 1lb peeled & deveined shrimp
[2] Whisk & set aside: 
  4 tbsp mayonnaise,
  1/4 tbsp Sriracha,
  1 tbsp rice vinegar,
  2 tbsp brown sugar
[3] Coat: #1, 3 tbsp cornstarch
[4] Fry on medium-high heat: #3, 1/4" oil
[5] Prepare: 2 cups rice (1/2 cup per serving)
[6] Dice: 1 cup cherry tomatoes
[7] Chop: 2 green onions
[6] Combine & serve: #2, #4, #5, #6, #7
</Recipe>

# Modifications
For a spicier version with more "bang", double the Sriracha.
